Where and How to Get a Card?

Step 1: Go to the Station

Look for a ticketing booth or Pasaheroute card section.

Step 2: Ask for a Card

Tell the cashier you want to buy a Pasaheroute card.

Step 3: Pay the Fee

The card costs ₱30, and you need to load at least ₱70 for an initial balance (total ₱100).

Step 4: Receive Your Pasaheroute Card

The cashier will give you the card with the loaded balance.

Step 5: Check Your Card Balance

You can check it at the booth or through the Pasaheroute card reader at the station entrance.
